5 Tricks to Ace CBSE Math Exams

Math isn't about memorizing; it's about strategy. Here is how toppers in Kurukshetra score full marks.

Why Students Fear Math?

In our experience at Unnat Tuition Center (Didar Nagar), we see many students terrified of Calculus or Trigonometry. But the fear usually comes from weak basics, not the difficulty of the subject.

Trick 1: Master the NCERT First

Many students rush to buy R.D. Sharma or R.S. Aggarwal. While these are good, 90% of the CBSE board paper comes directly from NCERT examples and exercises. Don't touch side books until you have solved every NCERT question twice.

Trick 2: Step-Marking is Your Friend

Did you know CBSE gives marks for steps? Even if your final answer is wrong, you can get 3 out of 4 marks just for writing the correct formula and substitution.

  • Always write the "Given" values.
  • Write the formula used in a box.
  • Show calculations clearly.

Trick 3: The 15-Minute Reading Time

Use the initial 15 minutes wisely. Identify the questions you know 100% and do them first. This builds confidence and creates a "good impression" on the examiner checking your sheet.

Trick 4: Visuals Matter

In Geometry and Mensuration, always draw a diagram. Even if the question doesn't ask for it, a neat pencil diagram shows you understand the concept.

Trick 5: Practice Previous Year Papers

At Unnat, we make students solve the last 10 years of papers. This is the only way to understand the pattern. If you are stuck, our teachers are here to help you solve them one-on-one.
