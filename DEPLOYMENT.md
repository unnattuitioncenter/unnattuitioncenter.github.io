# How to Host on GitHub (Step-by-Step)

You requested GitHub, so here is the simplest way to do it.

## Step 1: Find Your Files
First, open the folder with your website files.
1.  Open your **File Explorer**.
2.  Copy and paste this path into the top address bar:
    `C:\Users\MY PC\.gemini\antigravity\scratch\unnat_tuition_center`
3.  Keep this window open.

## Step 2: Create Repository
1.  Log in to [github.com](https://github.com/).
2.  Click the **+** (plus icon) in the top-right -> **New repository**.
3.  **Repository name**: Type `unnat-tuition`.
4.  Select **Public**.
5.  Scroll down and click **Create repository**.

## Step 3: Upload Files (The Smart Way)
1.  On the next screen, look for the link that says: **"uploading an existing file"**. Click it.
2.  Now, go back to your **File Explorer** window (from Step 1).
3.  **Select EVERYTHING** in that folder (Ctrl+A).
    *   (Make sure you select `index.html`, `style.css`, `images` folder, etc.)
4.  **Drag and Drop** all selected files into the GitHub browser window.
    *   *Wait for the progress bars to finish.*
5.  Scroll down to the "Commit changes" box.
6.  Click the green **Commit changes** button.

## Step 4: Turn on the Website
1.  Click the **Settings** tab (gear icon) at the top of your repository.
2.  On the left sidebar, click **Pages** (under "Code and automation").
3.  Under **Build and deployment**:
    *   **Source**: Choose "Deploy from a branch".
    *   **Branch**: Select **main** (or `master`) and folder **/(root)**.
4.  Click **Save**.

## Step 5: You are Online!
1.  Wait 1 minute.
2.  Refresh the page.
3.  You will see your link at the top (e.g., `https://yourname.github.io/unnat-tuition/`).
4.  Click it to see your "Premium Professional" website!
